# What is sbsepul.dev?

Looks like I'm starting to tell my own autobiography, but that's not my intention.
The reason I decided to launch my personal site is not just to showcase the projects and work I've done,
but above all I hope this is a space where I can share my thoughts, ideas, and knowledge 
for an open dialogue with anyone interested in these topics.

## What is sbsepul.dev?

{/* ## ¿Qué es sbsepul.dev? */}

This site is built with [Astro](https://astro.build/), React, and Tailwind CSS, and hosted on [Vercel](https://vercel.com/).
I chose to use these technologies for their ease of use and development speed.
The source code for this site is available in my [GitHub repository](https://www.github.com/sbsepul/github.sbsepul.com).

The design of this site is based on the work of [Kyle Kettler](https://www.kylekettler.com/) where I loved the
simplicity and cleanliness in the presentation of information, thanks Kyle for sharing your work.
